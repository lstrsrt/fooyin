# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behaviour

- Assume good intent; give constructive, actionable feedback.
- Keep conversations focused on the work; critique ideas, not people.
- Protect privacy and confidentiality: do not share secrets, tokens, personal data, or private content.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, threats, hateful or sexually explicit language, doxxing, or sustained disruptive conduct.
- Posting or attempting to share sensitive data, credentials, or other secrets in public channels, issues, or pull requests.
- Coercion, intimidation, or attempts to pressure contributors or maintainers.

## Reporting

- For conduct concerns, contact the maintainer(s) directly or open a private report via the repository’s communication channels.

## Enforcement

- Maintainers may remove or edit violating content, issue warnings, or restrict participation.
- For incidents involving security or sensitive data exposure, immediate action may be taken, including revoking access or removing affected content.
- Reports are handled confidentially and shared only on a need-to-know basis.
